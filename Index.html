    How to put downloaded configs into your macro

    1. Download the .txt file in one of the various config channels.
    2. Go to your downloads, and copy the file you just downloaded.
    3. Enter your macro folder, and go into the "Settings" sub folder.
    4. If your respective config is for a legend stage, dungeon, or raid you would go into their respective sub folders and paste it in there. If it is not, or is labeled "Custom.txt" then just paste it in the settings sub folder.
    5. Open up the macro, select the respective mode and your config will be there!

    Note: If your config is a raid, legend stage, or portal you must start in the lobby. Otherwise, your config must start in game (including custom).
